What We Do

Epoxy Flooring Services

From residential garages to large commercial facilities — we coat it all with precision and durability.

🏠

Garage Floor Epoxy

Transform your garage with a high-gloss, chip-resistant epoxy coating. Protects against oil, stains, and heavy foot traffic. Available in dozens of colors and flake styles.

🏭

Commercial Epoxy Coating

Heavy-duty industrial-grade coatings for warehouses, retail spaces, auto shops, and commercial kitchens. Built to handle forklifts, heavy equipment, and constant use.

✨

Metallic Epoxy Floors

Stunning swirled metallic finishes that turn any floor into a work of art. Perfect for showrooms, restaurants, and high-end residential spaces.

🔒

Polyaspartic Coating

UV-resistant, fast-curing polyaspartic topcoats that won't yellow in the Nevada sun. Ideal for outdoor patios, pool decks, and areas with direct sunlight exposure.

🏊

Pool Deck Coating

Slip-resistant, heat-reflective coatings for concrete pool decks. Keep surfaces cool underfoot in the Henderson heat while adding years of protection.

🔧

Concrete Prep & Repair

Professional diamond grinding, crack repair, and surface preparation before every coating — the foundation of a floor that lasts for decades.
